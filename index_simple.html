Welcome to the Demetori Appreciation Website!

"Bless you, Demetori, for bringing us closer to the very definition of quality heavy metal instrumental arranges that stems from the timeless finesse of Touhou's musical scores."
Site Admin

The longrunning yet obscure video game franchise titled Touhou and the similarly obscure metal circle Demetori have a fruitful relationship
as Demetori's members do not write their own music but base all their composition off the musical scores that the japanese video game has to offer.

Demetori is a band comprised of only two brothers: Kyuhouji (Alias of: Tadashi Teramae) and Tokunan (Alias of: Masaru Teramae).
The band was formed in 2005 and it works exclusively within the "Touhou Project" arrange genre,
The line-up consists of Kyuhouji playing the drums and Tokunan working with the guitar, bass and keyboard.
meaning that it only arranges music themes found within the game and releases full-length cover albums of this so-called "Touhou Metal".

In essence, Demetori is considered to be a progressive / powermetal band but it also delves a bit into the genre of symphonic metal.
Due to the fact that the band only has two members, Demetori makes use of guest players during live concerts and gigs.

Even if it isn't the first band to act purely as a music band offering arranges, Demetori peaks high above the others with its smashing guitar riffs,
technical proweness and mesmerizing musical interpretations of the video game's themes,
resulting in timeless arrangements that brings not only new fans to the game series but also fans to the metal genre.
