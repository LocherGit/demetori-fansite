At long last, le Grimoire De reve hits the shore with Demetori at the front while swinging guitars and drum sticks!
Included are the finalized version of Old Yuanxian and Heartfelt Fancy that were released in the previous Mini-Album.
But even without them, Demetori has plenty in store as they deliver with Charming Domination and Casket of Star, nostalgic tunes all around
while they dig deeper into the musical history of Touhou. It's been awhile since Demetori released something to look out for!
