2012 had been a rather disappointing year for Demetori-fans out there as the music circle announced a mini album for that year.
Tendre est la Mort consists of only two tracks if the Introduction is neglected. Though Demetori delivered with Old Yuanxian
as a classic metal hit and Heartfelt Fancy as a liberating rock ballad, fans throughout the globe anticipated too much to be satisfied
with only that. Demetori apologized in public for the lack of compositions and teased their next full-length album for 2013.
