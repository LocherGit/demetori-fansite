For most fans, Kijin Shokuaku ~Evil People as the True Object of Salvation~ felt like a gift straight from heaven.
Demetori took a one-year break to focus on dealing with problems that arose during that period of time and later released their album
at the Summer Comiket which was quite unusual for them, given their track record of releasing their flagships at past Winter Comikets.
Nevertheless, with Demetori's music came back the sense of longing that fans felt, a slight emotion of nostalgia that overcame those who hold
their musical influence dear. Demetori's presence at the Summer Comiket meant a lot for its commitment and passion to bring quality arranges.
