Higankasou ~the view of spiral riverside~ marks the first commercial album release of Demetori in cooperation with fellow music artist UI-70. This is the reason why the track numbers 01, 05, 09 and 10 are songs by UI-70.
Due to the nature of it being an indie-release, the sound quality of the album is quite meager yet profound conceptions of the band can be found.
