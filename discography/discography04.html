Il mondo dove e finito il tempo erupts into a colorful sea of twirling and speedy guitar melodies, blassbeats and complex rhythms.
Especially the increased tempo found within Jehovah's YaHVeH and Necrofantasia makes fans of Speedmetal blush in excitement.
But even in the case when Speedmetal isn't enough to please everyone, culturally infused tracks such as Chinese Tea or Ancient Temple
will do their share and bring out emotions that transcends the scale of auditory enjoyment. Please let yourself be entranced by this album.
Track 11 and 12 were included in a reprinted album that was later released at Reitaisai 8.
