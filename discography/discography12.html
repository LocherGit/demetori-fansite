Determinism&DestruKction is the latest album release of Demetori which marks the music circle as still being active on composing new Touhou arranges. It's more than likely that this will continue for quite some time even when some franchises such as Kantai Collection, GranBlue Fantasy and VocaLoids in general enjoy an evergrowing community and increasing amount of fan-arranges.
This album presents itself far more than a mere ordinary arrange album as Demetori once again steps it up a notch with complex rhythms and insane guitar sections working together with a beasty sounding drum.
