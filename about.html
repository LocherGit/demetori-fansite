About this Website:

Welcome to the Demetori Appreciation Website!

This website was created solely for the Web-Development lecture led by Prof. Heuzeroth of HdM Stuttgart.
My Alias on this Website is Ton Ngoc That and I humbly consider myself the Site Admin.

Though sparsely filled, I do feel proud to have created such a fantastic fansite for my beloved music circle and I dearly hope that the visitors will feel the same. I sincerely believe that the capabilities of doujin-created music far precede the reputation of those performing music circles.
My dearest wish is to give them the attention they deserve as to open said music to the public audience.

You too can do your part in order to make this kind of music more accessible to everyone!
